Safety training information

Independent lab users must complete training modules with VT EHS and sign a paper copy of the Training Documentation form, which is located with the safety documentation in each lab space. For reference, a copy of this form is also found on page 64 of the University Chemical Hygiene plan (CHP). Please let Lowell know if you have any questions or run into technical issues.

EHS training modules

Here is the website where you can register for lab safety training: https://www.ehss.vt.edu/train.php

Here is a list of the required EHS training courses:

  • (All lab users) Laboratory Safety > General Laboratory Safety
  • (All lab users) Fire and Life Safety > Portable Fire Extinguishers
  • (XRF users only) Radiation Safety > Analytical X-ray Equipment

Please note that all of the required training courses can be completed online, and they take roughly 30 minutes on average to complete. Information about the safety training modules that have been previously completed can be found here: https://www.ehss.vt.edu/profile.php. As you finish each course, please send Lowell a screenshot showing your "training completion record."
