Standard operating procedures (SOPs)

Note: The SOPs are intended to assist the lab manager and other more experienced users with training exercises, and are not intended to be used as a substitute for training. That is, if you forget how to do something, then refer to the SOP. If you want to do something new, DO NOT refer to the SOP -- instead, reach out to the lab manager (Lowell) or another more experienced user for training.
